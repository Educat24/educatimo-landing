Scientific basis

Classic approaches adapted for online delivery

The platform is built on approaches used for decades in neuropsychology, child psychology, and pedagogical diagnostics. Tasks are adapted for online delivery and different age groups while preserving the diagnostic logic of classic methods for assessing attention, memory, thinking, self-regulation, and self-esteem.
